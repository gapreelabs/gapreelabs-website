Reading Technology & Library Systems

Work on cutting-edge library management software, AI-powered book recommendation engines, and digital reading platforms that help children discover the joy of reading.

What You'll Work On:
  • Develop features for our library management system (cataloging, circulation, analytics)
  • Build AI recommendation algorithms based on reading levels and interests
  • Create engaging UI/UX for children's reading apps
  • Design gamification features to encourage reading habits
  • Analyze reading data to generate insights for educators
Who Should Apply:
  • Computer Science / IT / Software Engineering students
  • Skills: Python, JavaScript, React, Node.js, databases (SQL/NoSQL)
  • Interest in AI/ML, NLP, or recommendation systems (bonus)
  • Passion for literacy and children's education

Music Education Technology

Build interactive learning tools, gamified practice apps, and music center management systems that make music education accessible and engaging for all ages.

What You'll Work On:
  • Develop interactive music learning games and practice tools
  • Create student progress tracking and assessment systems
  • Build music center management platforms (scheduling, payments, attendance)
  • Design ear training and music theory modules
  • Work on content delivery systems for video lessons and sheet music
Who Should Apply:
  • Computer Science / IT / Software Engineering students
  • Skills: Web/mobile development, audio processing basics
  • Music background (Western/Carnatic/Hindustani) is a plus
  • Interest in EdTech and gamification

STEM & Robotics Product Development

Design and develop custom coding platforms, educational hardware, robotics kits, and hands-on learning tools that prepare students for the future.

What You'll Work On:
  • Build block-based and text-based coding platforms for kids
  • Design affordable educational robotics kits and hardware
  • Create computational thinking activities and curriculum
  • Develop IoT and maker kit projects for schools
  • Work on competition preparation tools (WRO, FLL)
Who Should Apply:
  • Computer Science / Electronics / Robotics / Mechatronics students
  • Skills: Python, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, hardware prototyping
  • Experience with robotics competitions (bonus)
  • Passion for making STEM accessible to all children

Educational Research & Innovation

Conduct research on learning methodologies, analyze educational data, and develop evidence-based curricula that improve student outcomes.

What You'll Work On:
  • Research learning science, cognitive development, and pedagogy
  • Analyze student performance data to generate insights
  • Design and test new curriculum approaches
  • Conduct field studies in schools and learning centers
  • Develop assessment frameworks and evaluation tools
Who Should Apply:
  • Education / Psychology / Statistics / Data Science students
  • Skills: Research methodology, data analysis, statistical tools
  • Interest in learning science and cognitive psychology
  • Strong writing and presentation skills

Content Development & Curriculum Design

Create engaging educational content, design curriculum, produce video lessons, and develop teaching materials across reading, music, and STEM domains.

What You'll Work On:
  • Design age-appropriate curriculum and lesson plans
  • Create video tutorials, practice exercises, and learning modules
  • Write educational blog posts, guides, and parent resources
  • Develop activity sheets, worksheets, and hands-on projects
  • Design visual content, infographics, and learning aids
Who Should Apply:
  • Education / Mass Communication / Design / English students
  • Skills: Content writing, curriculum design, video production/editing
  • Experience with graphic design tools (Canva, Adobe Suite) is a plus
  • Passion for creating engaging learning experiences

How to Apply

Internship Details

Duration: 3 to 6 months

Location: In-office (Bengaluru)

Application Status: Rolling applications

Stipend: Competitive stipend

Application Process:
  1. Submit Your Application: Send your resume, cover letter, and portfolio (if applicable) to info@gapreelabs.com
  2. Initial Screening: Our team will review your application within 5-7 business days
  3. Technical/Skills Assessment: Shortlisted candidates will receive a task relevant to their chosen track
  4. Interview: Final round interview with the team
  5. Offer: Selected candidates receive an offer letter with internship details
What to Include in Your Application:
  • Updated resume with relevant coursework and projects
  • Cover letter explaining why you want to intern at Gapreelabs and which track interests you
  • Portfolio or GitHub links (for technical roles)
  • Any relevant certifications or achievements
  • Available start date and duration
